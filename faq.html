Frequently Asked Questions

What prerequisites do I need for this program?

Basic computer skills and familiarity with web browsing are recommended. No prior AWS experience is required as we start from the fundamentals and build up your knowledge progressively.

How long is the program and what's the schedule?

The program runs for 12 weeks with flexible scheduling options. Classes are held twice a week in the evenings to accommodate working professionals, with additional weekend sessions available.

What technologies will I learn?

You'll master core AWS services including EC2, S3, RDS, Lambda, VPC, IAM, CloudFormation, and more. The curriculum covers cloud architecture, security, automation, and best practices for enterprise deployments.

Is there job placement assistance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive career support including resume review, interview preparation, and connections to our network of hiring partners. Our career services team works with you throughout and after the program.

What's the cost and payment structure?

The program fee is $2,999 with flexible payment options available including installment plans. Early bird discounts and scholarships are available for qualifying candidates.

Do I get a certificate upon completion?

Yes, you'll receive a certificate of completion and be prepared to take AWS certification exams. We also provide exam vouchers and additional preparation materials for AWS certifications.

Will I get hands-on experience with real projects?

Absolutely! The program includes multiple hands-on labs, real-world projects, and a capstone project where you'll build and deploy a complete cloud solution using AWS services.

Can I attend if I have a full-time job?

Yes, the program is designed for working professionals. Classes are scheduled in the evenings and weekends, with recorded sessions available for review if you miss a live class.

What if I don't have AWS credits for practice?

We provide AWS credits for all students to practice and complete assignments. You'll also learn how to use AWS Free Tier effectively to minimize costs during your learning journey.

What career paths can I pursue after completing the program?

Graduates can pursue roles as Cloud Engineers, Solutions Architects, DevOps Engineers, Cloud Security Specialists, or Cloud Consultants. The skills are applicable across all industries adopting cloud technologies.

Is the training conducted online or in-person?

We offer both online and hybrid options. Online classes feature live instruction with interactive labs, while hybrid options include in-person workshops and networking events in select cities.

Are there any hidden costs or additional fees?

No hidden fees. The program fee includes all course materials, AWS credits for practice, access to our learning platform, and career support services. Certification exam fees are separate but we provide vouchers.
